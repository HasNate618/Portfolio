## Description

A mobile adaptation of Ultimate Tic Tac Toe (also known as Super Tic Tac Toe or Meta Tic Tac Toe). Players compete in a strategic version of the classic game with nested boards.

### Game Rules

- 9x9 grid divided into 9 smaller 3x3 boards
- Winning a small board claims it for that player
- Three claimed small boards in a row wins the game
- Where you play determines which board your opponent must play in next
- Adds strategic depth to classic Tic Tac Toe
