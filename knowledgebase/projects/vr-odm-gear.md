# VR ODM Gear Sim

## Overview
VR simulation of Attack on Titan's ODM Gear with custom Arduino glove controller and Google Cardboard.

## Category
Games, VR, Hardware

## Status
Hackathon Project (Hack the North 2023)

## Tech Stack
- Unity3D
- C#
- Google Cardboard SDK
- Arduino Nano
- Bluetooth
- Blender, SolidWorks

## Description

An immersive VR experience that simulates the Omni Directional Movement (ODM) Gear from Attack on Titan. Players navigate a breached city using grappling hooks and thrusters to defeat Titans.

### The ODM Gear

The ODM Gear consists of:
- Gas-powered thrusters
- Retractable grappling hooks
- Harness system
- Two thrusters attached to left and right waist

Players swing through the air with incredible speed and precision, targeting Titan weak spots while avoiding attacks.

### Gameplay

- Navigate city under siege using ODM Gear
- Cross walls to reach Titan
- Defeat Titan before it destroys city wall
- Requires agility and fast thinking
- Grapple to buildings and swing through city

### VR Implementation

- Google Cardboard SDK for mobile VR
- Compatible with Google Cardboard headset
- Arduino-powered glove controller
- Real-time motion tracking

### Hardware Controller

**Arduino Nano Glove:**
- Integrated motion sensors
- Bluetooth connectivity
- Button input
- Accelerometer values track orientation
- Perfect for VR controller

### Development

**Game Engine**: Unity3D
**VR SDK**: Google Cardboard
**Controller**: Arduino Nano with sensors and Bluetooth
**Assets**: Mix of self-modeled (SolidWorks, Blender) and internet sources
**Physics**: Custom calculations for grapple and thruster mechanics

### Challenges

- **Physics Simulation**: Complex grapple and thruster mechanics
- **Optimization**: Balancing realistic physics with fun gameplay
- **City Design**: Non-linear level design for engaging traversal
- **VR Comfort**: Minimizing motion sickness

### Accomplishments

- Scalable game playable with cardboard and Arduino
- Fun, engaging gameplay
- Hardware-software integration
- First VR game for all team members
