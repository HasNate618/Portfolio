UWO Mealplan Calc (crhome extension bilt with js)
Extension
Overview

Calculates your daily mealplan spendings and your projected remaining balance by the end of the year.

Western Mealplan Calculator helps Western University students take control of their campus meal spending. Instantly analyze your daily purchases, track balances for all tender types (Residence Dollars, Flex Dollars, and more), and see clear projections for your remaining funds by year-end. Get actionable recommendations to optimize your budget, avoid overspending, and make smarter top-up decisions.
